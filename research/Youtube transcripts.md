## Video 1 — How To Create A Sales Funnel For FREE: Step-by-Step Tutorial

**Channel:** Gillian Perkins
**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Oq86E5cb6oI
**Published:** May 10, 2022
**Tool used:** MailerLite (free)

### What She Covers

Gillian walks through building a complete working sales funnel from scratch using only free tools. The tutorial starts at 6:08 and covers every stage hands-on.

**Chapter Breakdown:**

| Timestamp | What Is Covered |
|-----------|----------------|
| 0:00 | Intro — why every business needs a funnel |
| 2:09 | What tools you need to build one |
| 6:08 | Create your accounts |
| 6:47 | Create your products |
| 7:05 | Create the checkout page |
| 7:35 | Create your website |
| 8:08 | Create an email opt-in |
| 8:50 | How to get people on your email list |
| 9:25 | How to create an opt-in form |
| 11:03 | Create a sales page (optional) |
| 11:58 | Create an email campaign |
| 13:11 | Add a webinar (optional add-on) |

### Key Takeaways

- A full working funnel — checkout page, opt-in, email campaign, and optional webinar — can be built in under an hour using MailerLite for free
- The webinar is treated as an optional add-on at the bottom of the funnel, not the entry point — you build the email infrastructure first, then layer the webinar on top
- The opt-in form is the most important element — without it, visitors leave and never come back
- The email campaign is what moves people from "interested" to "ready to buy" — the pages alone don't convert, the sequence does

### Why This Is Relevant to 100Hires

This video confirms that the technology barrier to launching a webinar funnel is zero. 100Hires doesn't need expensive tools to test the model. MailerLite + a basic landing page is enough to run the first webinar, collect data, and validate before investing in a full stack.

## Video 2 — ULTIMATE Guide To Building A Sales Funnel (For Free)

**Channel:** Jason Whaling
**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lavvYMzjs08
**Published:** August 20, 2024
**Tool used:** Systeme.io (free)
**Duration:** 3 hours 8 minutes

### What He Covers

Jason builds an entire end-to-end funnel live — landing page, sales letter, checkout, upsell, email automation, and membership site — all using Systeme.io which is completely free. This is the most comprehensive free funnel build tutorial available.

**Chapter Breakdown:**

| Timestamp | What Is Covered |
|-----------|----------------|
| 0:00 | Intro |
| 0:38 | Funnel Blueprint — full architecture overview |
| 11:55 | Landing Page — opt-in page build |
| 46:58 | Sales Letter — long form copy structure |
| 1:29:37 | Checkout Form — payment and order setup |
| 1:56:01 | Upsell Page — post-purchase offer |
| 2:36:42 | Email Automation — sequences and triggers |
| 2:52:05 | Membership Site — gated content delivery |
| 3:05:49 | Outro |

### Key Takeaways

- The **Funnel Blueprint (0:38)** is the most important section — Jason maps the full architecture before touching any tool. Design the flow on paper first, build second.
- The **Sales Letter (46:58)** section reveals that most people skip long-form copy because it feels like too much work — but it is the single highest-converting page in the funnel for considered purchases like B2B SaaS
- The **Email Automation section (2:36:42)** is directly applicable to this research — Jason shows how to set triggers based on actions (opened email, clicked link, visited page) not just time. Behavior-based triggers outperform time-based ones significantly
- The **Upsell Page (1:56:01)** is the most underused element in B2B SaaS funnels — most companies just send buyers to a dashboard. A structured upsell immediately post-purchase can add 20-40% to revenue per customer

### Why This Is Relevant to 100Hires

The email automation section (2:36:42) maps directly to what 100Hires needs. Jason shows how to tag buyers vs non-buyers, trigger different sequences based on behavior, and automate the entire post-webinar follow-up. This is the same logic used in the funnel architecture documented in `/research/other/funnel-map.md` — just implemented in Systeme.io instead of n8n.

The upsell page framework is directly applicable — after a customer buys the basic 100Hires plan from a webinar, a structured post-purchase upsell to annual billing or a higher tier is one of the highest-ROI additions to the funnel.
